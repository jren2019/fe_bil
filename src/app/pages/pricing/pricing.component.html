Frequently Asked Questions

How are seats and runs counted?

Seats are counted as the number of humans using AssetX (Pro) in your account. Bot tokens (API keys) are not counted. Runs are the number of CI runs and deployments reported to your AssetX Cloud account in a given month.

Is AssetX Cloud SOC2 compliant?

Yes, we are proudly SOC2 compliant! We believe that in order to earn the trust of other organizations, in order for them to grant us the privilege of being infrastructure to their business, we must hold ourselves accountable to rigid standards. You can read more about this here, or if you need more information you can reach us at hello@ariga.io.
